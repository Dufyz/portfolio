About me

My name is Guilherme I. Schmidt THomaz, I’m from Brazil and I’m 18 years old.

Aside from my job, I like to create and contribute to open source projects. That helps me to learn a ton of new stuff, grow as a developer and support other open source projects.

I started as a Back-end Developer. Since then I have been dedicating myselfie to studies.

Codes, movies, video games, music, podcast and studies are subjects that interest me.

I see that I am on the right path because I love helping people who are starting out, just as I was a beginner. Furthermore, I see the results that the dedicated hours bring.

In my spare time I usually spend my time with family and friends.

I work as Back-end developer using Python, JavaScript, GO and some framework of them.
